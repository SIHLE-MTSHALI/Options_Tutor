## Trading Simulation

### Strategy Builder

The Strategy Builder allows you to create and test various options strategies:

#### Supported Strategies

**1. Covered Call**
- Own 100 shares + sell call option
- Generate income from premium
- Limited upside, downside protection from premium

**2. Cash-Secured Put**
- Sell put option + hold cash for assignment
- Acquire stock at discount if assigned
- Generate income while waiting

**3. Collar**
- Own stock + sell call + buy put
- Limited upside and downside
- Protective strategy for existing holdings

**4. Custom Strategies**
- Build multi-leg strategies
- Combine different option types
- Test complex scenarios

#### Strategy Creation Process

1. **Select Strategy Type**: Choose from templates or create custom
2. **Set Parameters**: Strike prices, expiration dates, quantities
3. **Review Setup**: Validate strategy configuration
4. **Analyze Payoff**: View profit/loss scenarios
5. **Execute Trade**: Place the simulated trade

### Error Messages

#### "Connection Failed"
- **Cause**: Network connectivity issues
- **Solution**: Check internet connection and firewall settings

#### "Data Unavailable"
- **Cause**: Market data service interruption
- **Solution**: Wait for service restoration or try refreshing

#### "Invalid Order"
- **Cause**: Order parameters don't meet requirements
- **Solution**: Review order details and market conditions

#### "Insufficient Funds"
- **Cause**: Not enough cash or margin for the trade
- **Solution**: Reduce position size or add funds

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Options Tutor free to use?**
A: Options Tutor offers both free and premium features. The basic educational content and limited trading simulation are free. Premium features include advanced analytics, unlimited positions, and priority support.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, Options Tutor uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. No real money or personal financial information is stored in the application.

**Q: Can I use real money for trading?**
A: No, Options Tutor is a simulation platform for educational purposes only. All trades are simulated with virtual money.

**Q: How accurate is the market data?**
A: Market data is sourced from reliable providers like Alpha Vantage and is updated in real-time during market hours. Options pricing uses the Black-Scholes model for educational accuracy.

### Educational Questions

**Q: I'm a complete beginner. Where should I start?**
A: Start with the "Options Fundamentals" tutorial in the Learning section. This will give you a solid foundation before moving to more advanced topics.

**Q: How long does it take to complete all tutorials?**
A: The complete tutorial series takes approximately 2-3 hours to complete, but you can learn at your own pace and return to any section as needed.

**Q: Do I get a certificate for completing tutorials?**
A: While there's no formal certification, you earn achievements and XP that track your progress and demonstrate your knowledge level.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What internet speed do I need?**
A: A stable broadband connection (5 Mbps or higher) is recommended for optimal real-time data updates.

**Q: Can I use Options Tutor on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, Options Tutor is a desktop application. Mobile and web versions are planned for future releases.

**Q: How often is the application updated?**
A: We release updates monthly with new features, improvements, and bug fixes. The application will notify you when updates are available.

**Q: Can I backup my progress and data?**
A: Yes, you can export your portfolio data and learning progress. The application also automatically saves your progress locally.

### Trading Questions

**Q: How realistic are the trading simulations?**
A: The simulations use real market data and realistic pricing models to provide an authentic trading experience without real financial risk.

**Q: Can I test strategies with historical data?**
A: Yes, you can analyze how strategies would have performed using historical market data and backtesting features.

**Q: Are there limits on the number of positions I can hold?**
A: Free users can hold up to 10 positions simultaneously. Premium users have unlimited positions.

**Q: How do I know if my strategy is profitable?**
A: The platform provides comprehensive P&L tracking, performance metrics, and risk analysis to help you evaluate strategy effectiveness.
